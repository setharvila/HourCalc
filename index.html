Hour Calculation Tool

This tool is designed to help you calculate what time you need to clock out to acheive a set number of hours for a week.

This tool should be used only to calculate the last day of the week (ie. when you need less than 24 hours to meet your goal).

This project is in its infancy and may cause errors. Use at your own risk!
